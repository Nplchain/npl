ABOUT NPL

The First Public Blockchain Of The Non-performing Asset  Around The World

NPL (Non Performance Loan)  is the first public blockchain of the non-performing assets  around the world, which reestablished the trust and trading mechanism of non-performing assets and solve the problems of the industry with a new transaction model, (Utilizing the the blockchain technology in the financial industry, which is safe and reliable, process simplified, publicate and transparent, instant settlement and keep the data non manipulable.)  "Blockchain and non-performing assets'' which will synchronize the core data information in the non-performing asset ecosystem through the DLT. Everyone has the opportunity to cooperate with various asset holding institutions or individuals, whether on the issuance of non-performing assets, and confirmation of rights and registration, or issuer valuation, due diligence, design and subsequent transactions and smart contracts signature, are all conducted onchain. 

Blockchain technology is the future, and non-performing assets are the blue ocean which will bring new opportunities to the non-performing asset industry. NPL's blockchain concept and technological innovation also provide new development ideas for the whole industry.
